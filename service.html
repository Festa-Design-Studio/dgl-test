UX research & strategy design

Best for organizations and startups that need to:

Understand their users deeply, including those with trauma histories.

Validate product or service ideas before development with diverse user groups.

Create data-driven design strategies that consider emotional safety.

Implement trauma-informed research methodologies.

Product or service design

Best for organizations and startups that need to:

Create a new digital product or service from scratch with trauma-informed principles.

Improve an existing product or service significantly while considering user vulnerabilities.

Balance user needs with business goals while maintaining psychological safety.

Design products or services that empower and support users with varied experiences.

UI / UX design for web & mobile

Best for organizations and startups that need to:

Make their digital products and services more user-friendly and emotionally safe.

Create visually appealing interfaces that avoid triggering content.

Establish consistent design systems with clear content warnings.

Implement accessible design patterns that support user agency.
