Our Trauma-Informed Design Philosophy

We create inclusive, accessible, and trauma-informed digital solutions that empower positive change

Our principles

Safety first

We prioritize psychological and emotional safety in every interaction, creating environments where users feel secure and supported.

User control

Our designs empower users with choice and agency, allowing them to navigate experiences at their own pace.

Accessibility

We ensure our digital solutions are accessible to all, following universal design principles and WCAG guidelines.

Our implementation framework

Research & Understanding

  • Conduct thorough user research
  • Identify potential triggers
  • Map user journeys

Safety Analysis

  • Assess psychological safety needs
  • Evaluate potential risks
  • Develop mitigation strategies
  • Create safety guidelines

User-Centered Design

  • Incorporate user feedback
  • Design for agency and control
  • Create clear navigation paths
  • Implement content warnings

Inclusive Implementation

  • Follow accessibility standards
  • Use inclusive language
  • Provide multiple interaction options
  • Create diverse representations
